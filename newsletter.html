One honest minute. Every Wednesday.

A short public journal from Motheo. Not advice, not productivity, not a finished lesson — just one honest reflection from someone trying to live with intention, shared with people doing the same.

Who it’s for

For people living a little against the curve.

If you’re trying to take life with purpose — and actually act on it, rather than drift along with everyone else — this is a small weekly signal that you’re not doing it alone.

What it is

A journal that happens to be public.

Each issue is a single honest reflection on something real — an ordinary moment, an observation, a small shift in thinking — worked through on the page rather than packaged up as a lesson.

You’re not being taught or advised. You’re being let into a private train of thought that happens to be shared. Closer to reading someone’s journal entry that they decided to send, than content built to be useful.

  • One honest reflection each week — not a lesson, not a list of links.
  • Under a minute to read. That’s the whole promise, and it’s in the name.
  • No advice, no productivity hacks, no performance. Just what’s actually going on.
  • For people trying to live with intention, against the drift.
