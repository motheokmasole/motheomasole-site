About Me

Engineering student.
Personal development writer.

I'm Motheo — originally from Botswana, now studying Mechanical Engineering at the University of Bath. Coming here was a dream of mine for years, but once I arrived, I faced something I didn't expect: the challenge of finding my purpose.

Over time, I've learned to slow down, appreciate where I am, and build small habits that keep me steady. I write about those lessons every week so you don't have to learn them the hard way.
