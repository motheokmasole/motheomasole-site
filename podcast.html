About the show

Real conversations.
No filter.

Being young is hard. Nobody really prepares you for what it actually feels like — the anxiety, the expectations, the pressure to have it all figured out before you even know who you are.

With Intention is for young people who want to live differently. With more purpose, more honesty, and more of themselves in every decision they make. Every episode we get into the real stuff — habits, relationships, mental health, values, the future.

Honest conversations with people who've figured something out, and solo reflections from someone still figuring it out. Think of it as an older brother who's a few steps ahead — and doing the work right alongside you.
