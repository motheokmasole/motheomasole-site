Why this exists

Most people your age aren’t short on ambition. They’re short on access.

Access to the right conversations. The right role models. The right kind of honesty about what it actually takes to live a life with purpose instead of just a busy one.

With Intention exists to close that gap — to put the conversations most people never get in the room for out in the open, every week.

To take your purpose back into your own hands.

What to expect
01

Conversations with people who’ve found clarity

Each week, Motheo sits down with experts, authors, and builders who’ve found real clarity in their own work — and pulls out what actually helped.

02

Solo episodes, thinking out loud

Between the interviews, reflective episodes where he works through whatever he’s wrestling with himself — a few steps ahead, not pretending to have arrived.

03

The questions that actually matter

Purpose, identity, and ambition: what to do when the path isn’t obvious, how to want things for the right reasons, and how to own your meaning instead of waiting for it.
